About Us

ROBO-TECH Team has been founded in November 2014 by some outstanding students studying Electrical and Mechanical Engineering at Alexandria University.

We participate in Robotics competitions such as Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) competitions and Minesweepers competitions.

We raise generations of college students and schools’ students; we teach them our knowledge and help them reach outstanding technical and non-technical levels of knowledge as part of our vision for a better education system.

Our Services

We have established a technical academy to teach technical concepts of robotics to school students under the age of eighteen, we provide supervision and guidance.

Teaching university students the basics for building their own ROV.

Building an industrial ROV that meets the needs of the market.

Aquatech application that allows the user to follow aquatics with recognition.


Increase knowledge about robotics field and its importance in Egypt, and attract more attention and support to it.
